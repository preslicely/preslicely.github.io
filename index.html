Our Story

Ever felt frustrated at the size of 'off-the-peg' pizza slices?

We have. And that's why we started Preslicely.

Based in central London, we make delicious, handmade pizza in our custom-built wood fired pizza oven, and sell it by the slice in 10° increments.

Preslicely: the only place that you can exercise your love of pizza and your love of angles at the same time.


Menu

Our pizza toppings use seasonal ingredients, so our menu changes throughout the year. This week we're serving:

  • Buffalo mozzarella, tomato, basil
  • Prosciutto cotto, wild mushrooms, tomato, thyme
  • Aubergine, tomato, scamorza, pesto
  • Salami, tomato, mozzarella, chilli flakes
  • Spicy sausage, mozzarella, broccoli, cream

All pizzas cost £0.60/10°. We recommend at least 90° per person.
